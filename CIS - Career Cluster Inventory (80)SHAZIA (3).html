    My CIS Portfolio

    Your portfolio houses all your personal information, your plans, and the things you have saved. This information all comes together to help you form an individual career plan.

    Your portfolio is portable and can be transferred to CIS sites across Illinois. Moving your portfolio helps you connect with new counselors and advisors easily; getting them up to speed on your plans.
